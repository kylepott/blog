Book Review: Divine Direction

November 18, 2017

[caption id="attachment_124” align="alignleft” width="225”] A book as big as your head![/caption]Divine Direction: 7 Decisions That Will Change Your Life written by Craig Groeschel broke down seven decisions you need to make: Stop, Start, Go, Stay, Serve, Connect, and Trust. The book interweaves well-known biblical stories to demonstrate aspects of the decision. Overall, this book was topical and a quick read, and a good tool to do some self reflection. The seven decisions, in general, are good things to keep in mind day-to-day. Unfortunately, the book lacks depth and substance. I can’t recommend it.
